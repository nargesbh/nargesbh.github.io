Grapevine Leaves Classification

This is a final project for the data mining course. Using pre-trained models and transfer learning, the aim is to classify the five classes of grapevine leaves. The used pretrained models are MobileNetV2, ResNet50, EfficientNetB3, and InceptionNetV3. We see the effects of autoencoders on the accuracy and use 10 fold cross validation as a measurement.
